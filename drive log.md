The drive log is to log what happens in our drive to help us improve our skill of drive.
It should have these properties 
- recording time (start-end)
- Overview review 
	-  good/bad/medium rate:
- bad events 
	- when it happens 
	- What happens 
	- Why it happens and reflection 
		- Mood 
			- analysis how the mood generates.
			- How to avoid or handle these mood
		- Skill
			- make a plan to improve the drive skill
- good events
	- when it happens 
	- What happens 
	- Why it happens  and reflection
		- Mood: 
			- analysis how the mood generates.
			- Enhance the good driving feeling 
		- Skill :
			- make a plan to improve the drive skill
